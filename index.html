Research · ML · Springer
Student Performance Prediction
Comparative analysis of four classification algorithms — ANN, Decision Tree, KNN, and Naïve Bayes — for university student performance detection, predicting CGPA from semester results and campus-facility survey reviews. Three feature-selection methods (Chi-Square, Euclidean, correlation) are combined into 22 shared features. 03
Algorithm · Optimization · Springer
TSP Genetic Algorithm — Mutation Triggering Method
Combines three mutation operators — Swap, Insertion, and 2-opt — in a generation-aware triggering method: random mutations in the first 80% of generations for speed and diversity, knowledge-based 2-opt in the final 20% for optimality. Benchmarked on 9 TSPLIB instances; MTM-GA found the best travel cost on 6 of 9. Published in Springer, Embracing Industry 4.0 (pp. 159–170).

Sightless Helper
An Android assistant for visually impaired users combining voice-driven interaction, accelerometer-based footstep counting for indoor navigation, GPS and Google Geofence for outdoor navigation and unsafe-area alerts, and a jolt module that auto-calls trusted contacts and sends a live-location SMS on detected falls. Tested with 30 users (SUS 72.27, "Good"); voice recognition ≈ 6.34 ms, location delivery ≈ 7.63 ms across distances from 1.5 km to 244 km.
